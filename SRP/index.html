Overview

UK:RP|SRP is a highly immersive, realism-focused emergency services roleplay experience set within a true 1:1 recreation of Shirebrook, designed to replicate real-world environments, operations, and decision-making as closely as possible.

Roles & Services

Players can take on a variety of roles across multiple emergency services and civilian pathways.

Roleplay & Progression

The core of UK:RP|SRP is its structured roleplay system, prioritising realism and communication.

Community & Events

An active staff team ensures the game remains fair, organised, and enjoyable.

Experience

Built for players who value realism, structure, and community.
