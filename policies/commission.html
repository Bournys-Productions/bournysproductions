Policies

Commission Resale Rights

Please be aware that Bournys Productions retains the right to resell any commission we create for you. When you purchase a commission, you are receiving a license to use the content, but we maintain ownership and may offer the same or similar products to other clients.

Turnaround Time

Standard commissions typically take 1-3 weeks depending on complexity. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee. We will provide you with an estimated completion date when your commission is confirmed.

Revisions

Each commission includes up to two rounds of minor revisions. Additional major changes may incur extra charges. We want you to be satisfied with the final product, so please communicate clearly about your requirements upfront.

Communication

We will keep you updated throughout the development process. You can expect regular progress updates and will have opportunities to provide feedback at key milestones.

File Delivery

Completed commissions will be delivered as Roblox-compatible files via the agreed-upon method, such as a Roblox model file or direct game integration. All necessary documentation and installation instructions will be included. Files will most likely be downloaded from a given URL.
