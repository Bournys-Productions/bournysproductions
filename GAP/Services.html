Services & Benefits

1. Overview

The Game Assist Program (GAP) provides structured support to developers through development assistance, media creation, and marketing services. The purpose of the Program is to improve game quality, visibility, and long-term growth through consistent support and promotion.

2. Development Services

Developers may receive guidance and feedback to improve gameplay systems, optimisation, structure, and overall game quality. This includes suggestions for mechanics, performance improvements, and general development direction where required.

3. Media Creation

The Program may include media support such as thumbnails, promotional images, branding assets, and short-form content. These assets are designed to improve presentation quality and help games appear more professional and appealing to players.

4. Marketing Services

Games within the Program may receive marketing support through Bournys Productions’ official Discord server and social media platforms. This includes promotional posts, announcements, and structured exposure to increase visibility and player engagement.

5. Social Media Promotion

Selected projects may be featured on Bournys Productions social accounts. This may include game highlights, updates, promotional posts, and community showcases designed to increase reach and awareness.

6. Program Benefits

Developers accepted into the Game Assist Program may receive free marketing exposure through Bournys Productions’ platforms. This includes access to promotional support that would otherwise require external advertising resources.

7. Growth Support

The Program is designed to support long-term growth by combining development assistance with active promotion. This helps developers improve game quality, attract new players, and increase retention over time.

8. Disclaimer

All services provided through the Game Assist Program are intended to support development and improve potential outcomes. No guarantees are made regarding revenue, player count, or overall game success.
