Terms & Conditions

1. Overview

The Game Assist Program ("Program") is provided by Bournys Productions to support developers through marketing, media creation, and development assistance. By joining, you agree to all terms outlined below.

2. Ownership

You retain 100% ownership of your game, including all assets, code, and intellectual property. Bournys Productions does not claim ownership at any time.

3. Partnership Types

Service Package: One-time payment for services. You keep full control and 100% revenue.

Publishing Partnership: No upfront cost. Bournys Productions provides support in exchange for 20% of game revenue during the partnership.

4. Publishing Rights

During a Publishing Partnership, Bournys Productions is granted rights to publish, promote, and monetise the game. The game may be hosted under Bournys Productions for visibility and management. You retain full development access at all times.

5. Revenue Share & Obligations

The 20% revenue share applies to all income generated by the game during the partnership.

Any attempt to avoid, redirect, or stop the agreed revenue share during the partnership or the 30-day post-exit period will be treated as a breach of agreement.

6. Breach of Terms

If revenue sharing or terms are violated, Bournys Productions reserves the right to:
- Immediately terminate the partnership
- Remove all support and promotion
- Restrict access to future programs
- Take appropriate action to recover unpaid amounts

7. Minimum Term

Publishing Partnerships require a minimum commitment of 30 days unless otherwise agreed.

8. Leaving the Program

You may leave at any time with 7 days notice.

If leaving before the minimum term:
- An agreed exit fee may apply, OR
- Revenue share continues for 30 days after exit

9. After Exit

Upon leaving, your game will be returned to your control and all publishing rights held by Bournys Productions will end. Ownership remains fully yours.

10. Developer Responsibilities

You must remain active, communicate when required, and follow all platform rules. Failure to do so may result in removal from the program.

11. No Guarantees

Bournys Productions does not guarantee player count, revenue, or success. All support is provided to improve potential outcomes only.

12. Changes to Terms

These terms may be updated at any time. Continued participation means you accept any updates.

13. Acceptance

By joining the Game Assist Program, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to all terms listed above.
